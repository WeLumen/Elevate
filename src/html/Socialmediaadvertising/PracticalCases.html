Lesson 4: Practical Cases

Restaurant "Honest Greens" (Instagram Ads)

Honest Greens, a restaurant that promotes healthy eating, launched an Instagram Ads campaign aimed at attracting an audience interested in healthy living and fresh products in several Spanish cities, such as Madrid and Barcelona. Through visual ads highlighting the freshness of their ingredients and their "farm-to-table" philosophy, they targeted young and professional users with an interest in conscious eating and fitness. The campaign included collaborations with influencers in the food and fitness sectors, who shared their experiences at Honest Greens' locations. Thanks to this strategy, the brand achieved a significant increase in Instagram followers, greater visibility in key cities, and a considerable boost in reservations.

Bicycle Shop "Santafixie" (Facebook and Instagram Ads)

Santafixie, an online urban bicycle shop based in Barcelona, launched a social media campaign to promote its range of customisable bikes. They used both Facebook and Instagram Ads, focusing on urban cyclists and people interested in sustainable mobility in major Spanish cities. Through video and carousel ads, they showcased the benefits of their bicycles and how to customise them. The geographical targeting focused on metropolitan areas such as Barcelona, Madrid, and Valencia, where cycling is becoming increasingly popular. The campaign was highly successful, boosting not only sales but also strengthening the brand's image as a modern and sustainable option for urban transport.

Craft Beer "Cervezas La Virgen" (Facebook and Instagram Ads + Live Events)

Cervezas La Virgen, a craft brewery in Madrid, used social media to promote both their products and their live tasting events. The campaign on Facebook and Instagram featured visually appealing posts showing the craft beer-making process. They also invited users to attend their tasting events and guided tours of their brewery. This campaign was mainly targeted at men and women aged 25 to 45, who are interested in beer culture, and located in Madrid. The strategy helped them establish themselves as one of the most popular craft beer brands in Spain, leading to an increase in event attendance and direct sales of their products both online and in stores.

Bakery "Turris" (Facebook Ads + Google Ads)

Turris, a chain of artisanal bakeries in Barcelona, implemented a campaign on Facebook Ads and Google Ads to attract more local customers to their shops. Through images showcasing the artisanal baking process and other bakery products, they connected with an audience that values quality and locally sourced products. The campaign was targeted at users aged 30 to 50, families, and those interested in artisanal and sustainable food, focusing on areas around their physical shops in Barcelona. By combining geographical targeting with local search ads on Google, Turris managed to increase foot traffic to their shops and direct sales of their artisanal products.

Marca de cosmética “Freshly Cosmetics” (Instagram Ads + Influencers)

Freshly Cosmetics, a natural cosmetics brand born in Spain, has carried out successful social media campaigns using Instagram Ads and influencer collaborations. They targeted their ads at women aged 18 to 40, interested in vegan and chemical-free beauty products. With a focus on conscious personal care and sustainability, their visual ads highlighted natural ingredients and customer reviews, alongside collaborations with key influencers in the beauty and wellness industry. These partnerships helped build trust and increase the brand's credibility, allowing them to expand their customer base not only in Spain but also internationally. Freshly Cosmetics experienced rapid growth thanks to this well-targeted strategy.
