Lesson 2: Defining Objectives

“The right content, to the right person, in the right place, at the right time.”

The definition of objectives should stem from:

  • Satisfying users
  • Meeting the company's objectives

It is the mission of the content strategist to ensure that the company makes its relevant content available to its users on any platform, in the way the consumer wishes to engage with it.

The user decides how, when, and where they consume it; companies must adapt.

Satisfying Users

To satisfy users, the content strategy should focus on:

Relevance:

  • Generating content that is relevant to the needs, interests, and problems of your consumers.
  • Staying updated with trends and topics of interest that attract users.
  • Identifying new content territories that could be effective in meeting our objectives.

Value:

  • Providing useful information, entertainment, inspiration, or education that adds real value to users' lives.
  • Offering solutions to specific problems faced by users.

Accessibility:

  • Ensuring that content is available on the platforms and formats preferred by users (mobile, computers, VOD, etc.).

Interaction:

  • Encouraging active user participation through comments, likes, sharing posts, or interacting via direct messages.
  • Responding to interactions in a timely and personalised manner to build a closer relationship.

Meeting the Company's Objectives

To meet the company's objectives, the content strategy must align with corporate goals, such as:

Increasing Brand Recognition:

  • Creating content that reinforces the identity and values of the brand.
  • Increasing brand visibility through communication campaigns and strategic collaborations.

Generating Engagement:

  • Creating content that invites interaction and connects emotionally with the audience.
  • Telling stories and narratives that resonate with users; we must know the emotional levers of our target audiences.

Encouraging Conversion:

  • Creating content that guides users through the sales funnel, from awareness to purchase.
  • Including clear and effective calls to action (CTAs) to drive conversions.

Retaining and Loyalty Building:

  • Providing exclusive and high-value content for existing customers; creating closed and exclusive communities is an effective way to make them feel special and provide real value.
  • Maintaining constant and relevant communication to foster brand loyalty.

Optimising ROI:

  • Measuring and analysing content performance to ensure a positive return on investment.
  • Adjusting strategies based on data and metrics to maximise efficiency.
