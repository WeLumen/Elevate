Social media advertising

E-learning Courses

This course is designed to equip professionals, entrepreneurs, and content creators with essential skills to thrive on social media and in the growing world of streaming. With a hands-on and up-to-date approach, the course is divided into four in-depth modules covering everything from content strategy to effective digital advertising and current trends on emerging platforms like Twitch and TikTok.


Blending theory with real-world application, this course offers case studies, practical examples, and actionable insights for anyone aiming to grow strategically and professionally in the digital space.

What Will You Learn?

Throughout this course, you will learn how to build effective social media strategies by understanding the importance of content, setting clear marketing objectives, and identifying your target audience. You’ll explore the main social platforms, learn how to create and manage paid advertising campaigns, and discover how to use audience segmentation to maximize performance.

The course will also teach you how to create engaging and relevant content, enhance brand engagement, and run successful promotions. Additionally, you will dive into the world of streaming, gaining insights into platforms like Twitch and TikTok, understanding current trends, and learning how to adapt your strategies for different demographics in the ever-evolving digital landscape.

Who Would Benefit from This Course?

This course is designed for a wide range of professionals and aspiring digital communicators who want to master social media and streaming strategies. Marketing professionals will benefit by gaining updated tools to plan campaigns, optimize audience targeting, and enhance brand visibility across platforms. Entrepreneurs and small business owners will learn how to position their brand online, attract new clients, and grow through organic and paid strategies. Content creators and influencers will gain practical knowledge on how to engage followers, create impactful content, and adapt to platform trends like those on TikTok and Twitch.

Students in the fields of communication, digital media, or business will find the course a valuable complement to their academic studies, offering real-world applications and case studies. Freelancers and consultants will be able to apply the strategies to support clients more effectively and expand their service offerings. Even professionals transitioning to digital marketing from other industries will benefit, as the course is structured to build a solid foundation while also exploring advanced concepts for implementation in diverse contexts.

By studying this course, you will:
Understand how social media and streaming platforms operate in the current digital landscape.
Learn to create strategic, engaging, and audience-focused content.
Define clear and measurable marketing objectives.
Analyze audience behavior and apply effective segmentation techniques.
Plan and manage paid advertising campaigns on platforms like Instagram, Facebook, TikTok, and Twitch.
Boost engagement and brand visibility through organic and paid strategies.
