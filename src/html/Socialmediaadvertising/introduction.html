Introduction

Welcome to your course Social media advertising. This course is divided into 4 modules.

Module 1

Discover the importance of well-planned content, learn how to set clear objectives, and analyze your audience to build a strong and effective digital presence.

Module 2

Dive into the main advertising platforms, explore different ad formats, and master audience segmentation. Real-world case studies will help you apply these strategies in a practical and impactful way.

Module 3

Understand the central role of social media today and learn how to create meaningful content, drive audience engagement, and leverage promotions to grow your reach and influence.

Module 4

Explore the rise of streaming content and how to tailor strategies for various demographic groups. Stay updated on the latest trends on platforms like Twitch and TikTok — and learn how to use them to your advantage.
