Lesson 5: Advertising and Promotions

Brands can enhance their content strategy on social media by complementing it with advertising campaigns and promotions that help to expand their reach and connect more deeply with their audience.

Social Media Advertising

By using paid advertising campaigns, brands can accurately segment their audience based on location, interests, behaviours, and other demographic data. These campaigns can boost existing content, ensuring it reaches new audiences who are not yet familiar with the brand. Facebook and Instagram Ads allow brands to target those who have interacted with previous posts or visited the website, maximising visibility and conversion of potential customers.

For companies with a local focus, geo-fencing campaigns can be activated to target audiences only when they are consuming content near the premises of interest.

Example

A small local clothing shop can create a campaign targeting women aged 25 to 35 within a 20 km radius. Using carousel ads that display products directly in the feed can encourage immediate purchases. The platform used for the campaign will be determined by the analysis of our audience. Perhaps the most suitable channel for this example would be Instagram, but it is advisable to review the channels where content is consumed, as these can change fairly quickly.

Promotions and Discounts

Promotions are effective tools to attract new followers and retain existing customers. Offering exclusive discounts, competitions, or vouchers through social media can increase engagement and generate more sales. Promotions like "2 for 1", "limited-time discount", or "free shipping" often work well, as they create a sense of urgency and exclusivity.

Example

A highly effective promotion is to run a giveaway, inviting users to participate by following the account, commenting, and sharing the post. This increases the brand’s organic visibility and helps foster an active and loyal community.

Without resorting to spam, this is a fairly common practice, especially in the restaurant industry. For example, “Tag the person you’d like to visit with and share this post on your stories” is a common tactic on Instagram. This helps reach new audiences and makes the brand known through connections that already exist. There are many tools available to help run giveaways and select winners.

Sumary

By combining targeted advertising with attractive promotions, brands can strengthen their social media strategy, achieving greater reach, engagement, and conversion. Social platforms allow real-time measurement of results, making it easier to optimise campaigns to maximise return on investment.
