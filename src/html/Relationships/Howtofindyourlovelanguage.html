Lesson 2: How to find your love language?

We all experience love in our own unique way—it’s the energy that nourishes our hearts, deepens our relationships, and makes us feel truly seen.

Understanding your love language can help you create more meaningful, fulfilling connections.

Not sure what yours is? Let’s explore it together with this soulful little quiz. Take a deep breath, tune into your heart, and choose the answers that resonate most with you.

It fills my heart with love when my partner...

  • A. Sets aside special time just for us, free from distractions.
  • B. Sends a heartfelt message, reminding me they’re thinking of me.
  • C. Thoughtfully does something to make my day easier.
  • D. Holds my hand, hugs me tightly, or snuggles up close.
  • E. Surprises me with a meaningful gift, chosen just for me.

For a special occasion like an anniversary, I love to...

  • A. Plan a beautiful getaway, just the two of us.
  • B. Write something heartfelt—a note, a love letter, or a meaningful post.
  • C. Do something kind, like making them breakfast in bed.
  • D. Create a deeply intimate moment, filled with touch and closeness.
  • E. Find the perfect gift that expresses my love and appreciation.

One of the most meaningful things a partner has done for me is…

  • A. Arranged a special experience or adventure for us.
  • B. Written me something from the heart, like a poem or a letter.
  • C. Made time for me, even when life was busy.
  • D. Held me close when I needed comfort.
  • E. Given me a gift that showed they truly understand me.

My idea of the perfect date is...

  • A. Something where we create beautiful new memories together.
  • B. A deep, uninterrupted conversation where we truly connect.
  • C. Doing something thoughtful, like volunteering together.
  • D. A couples massage or a cozy night wrapped in each other’s arms.
  • E. A thoughtful surprise, planned just for me.

One of the most beautiful parts of being in a relationship is...

  • A. Always having a companion to share life’s moments with.
  • B. Hearing loving words that uplift and affirm me.
  • C. Having someone who supports me through their actions.
  • D. The warmth of physical closeness, cuddles, and gentle touch.
  • E. Expressing love through thoughtful gifts and surprises.

Now, let’s reveal your love language…

  • Mostly A’s – Quality Time: You feel most loved when your partner is fully present with you. Deep conversations, shared experiences, and meaningful moments together fill your heart with joy.
  • Mostly B’s – Words of Affirmation: You cherish loving words, sweet messages, and verbal expressions of appreciation. A heartfelt “I love you” or an encouraging compliment makes your soul shine.
  • Mostly C’s – Acts of Service: Love, for you, is in the little things—thoughtful gestures, support in daily life, and small acts of kindness that show deep care.
  • Mostly D’s – Physical Touch: You connect most deeply through closeness—hugs, holding hands, and warm embraces make you feel truly loved and secure.
  • Mostly E’s – Receiving Gifts: It’s not about material things, but the thought and love behind them. A meaningful gift is a symbol of deep care, and you love to give and receive in this way.

Remember, we all have a beautiful blend of love languages. When we understand how we give and receive love, we open our hearts to more nurturing, fulfilling relationships.

Which love language speaks to your soul? Share in the comments below!
