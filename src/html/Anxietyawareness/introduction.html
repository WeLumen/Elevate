Introduction

Welcome to your courseAnxiety awareness & Stress Management. This course is divided into 11 modules.

Module 1: What is Anxiety?

In this introductory module, you will gain a clear understanding of what anxiety truly is. We'll explore how it develops, why it occurs, and the role it plays in our mental and physical health. This foundation will help you recognize anxiety in yourself and others with greater awareness.

Module 2: How Anxiety Can Make You Feel

Anxiety affects each person in unique ways. This module explores the emotional, physical, and cognitive sensations that often accompany anxiety, helping you identify these feelings and understand how they may influence your daily life.

Module 3: How People are Affected by Anxiety

Here, we take a closer look at how anxiety impacts different areas of life—from relationships to work performance. This module highlights the diverse ways anxiety shows up and how it can vary from person to person.

Module 4: Anxiety Triggers

In this module, you'll discover the common internal and external triggers that can provoke anxiety. By understanding what activates anxious responses, you’ll be better equipped to manage and respond to them in healthy ways.

Module 5: Approaches to Anxiety

There is no one-size-fits-all solution to anxiety. This module introduces various approaches—from therapeutic techniques to lifestyle changes—that can support emotional balance and reduce anxiety symptoms effectively.

Module 6: An Introduction to Stress

Stress and anxiety often go hand in hand. In this module, you'll explore what stress is, how it differs from anxiety, and why understanding the stress response is essential for overall mental wellness.

Module 7: Feeling Stressed

This module dives into the common emotional and physical signs of stress. You'll learn to recognize when stress is taking hold and how it might be affecting your energy, focus, and mood.

Module 8: How Stress Affects You

Stress can impact nearly every aspect of life. This module explains the wide-ranging effects stress can have on your body, mind, relationships, and behavior—empowering you with knowledge to take control.

Module 9: Mindfulness

Mindfulness is a powerful tool for managing stress and anxiety. In this module, you'll learn what mindfulness really means, how it works, and how you can use simple practices to bring calm and clarity into your everyday life.

Module 10: Emotional Freedom Techniques (EFT)

Discover EFT—a transformative method that combines gentle tapping with focused statements to release emotional blockages. This final module guides you through the basics of EFT and how it can be used to reduce anxiety and promote inner peace.
