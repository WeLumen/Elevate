Approaches to Anxiety

This lesson highlights methods of overcoming anxiety, as well as specific techniques that have been proven to be successful.

We begin by looking at various personality types and their links to anxiety before moving onto techniques that can help to overcome anxiety, such as diet, healthy lifestyle choices, controlled breathing and sharing thoughts with others.

Personality Types

Many studies have looked closely at the link between people’s personality types and the way people are able to deal with anxiety and stress...

  • Do you need to win in order to find enjoyment in games and sports?
  • Do you feel guilty if you use your spare time to relax?
  • Do you often try to multitask?
  • Do you generally move, walk, and eat rapidly?

Type A personalities

  • Competitiveness
  • Strong work ethic
  • Proactive
  • Anxious
  • Impatient
  • Many achievements
  • Sensitive
  • Ambitious
  • Strong organisational skills

Type B personalities

  • Calm personality
  • Adaptable
  • Steady workers
  • Reflective
  • Relaxed

How we think can affect how likely we are to suffer with Anxiety

Negative thinking makes people more likely to experience anxiety and this is something that comes naturally to some people more than others...

So, what can we do about Anxiety?

Are there ways that we can support ourselves and others?

"Absolutely!"

Exercising

Exercise releases hormones including serotonin; creating a positive feeling in the body and boosting mood...

Talking

People with anxiety often find it difficult to open up and talk about their feelings...

Diet

Diet can play a large part in making somebody feel healthy and well...

Avoiding Stimulants, Drugs and Alcohol

Stimulants, drugs, and alcohol can increase the heart rate, mimicking the symptoms of an anxiety attack...

Breathing Exercises

When we take deep breaths, we are getting more oxygen back to our brain...

  • Breathe in slowly for 5 seconds
  • Hold for 5 seconds
  • Breathe out slowly for 5 seconds

Three Minute Breathing Exercise

  • Minute 1: Become more aware of how you feel
  • Minute 2: Focus on your breath
  • Minute 3: Focus on your body and surroundings

Mindfulness

Practicing mindfulness, being present and ok with yourself, can really help reduce those feelings of anxiety...

Emotional Freedom Techniques

EFT, or Emotional Freedom Techniques, are a relatively new discovery but use age-old energy understanding and knowledge...

Getting a Hobby

Finding a hobby is a brilliant way to preoccupy a mind that is prone to anxiety...

Support Online

There are countless forms of support online, giving advice and making people feel as though they are not alone...

Medication Support

Medication can be given to those with anxiety that impacts their ability to enjoy a normal quality of life...

Groups in the Local Community

Community groups can be a brilliant way for people with anxiety to speak to others in similar situations...

  • Personality Types – How these can affect Anxiety.
  • Ways of Overcoming Anxiety – Support and self-help alternatives to medication.
