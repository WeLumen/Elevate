Lesson 1: Quit the Cycle of Self Sabotage

Is the voice that whispers “I’m not good enough” or “I’ll never make it” quietly holding you back, keeping the radiant woman you’re meant to be from fully stepping into her light?

Do you find yourself setting beautiful, heartfelt goals, only to hesitate, procrastinate, or let them drift away—feeling deep down like you’re not quite deserving of the success your soul longs for?

Are you caught in a tender but exhausting dance of self-sabotage—where just as you near a breakthrough, fear and doubt gently tug you backward?

Have you noticed yourself dimming your own brilliance—whether in your career, your relationships, or your personal growth—and feeling the weight of playing small when your spirit craves expansion?

If your heart whispered ‘yes’ to any of these questions, this mini-course was created for you, with so much love.

Inside this sacred space, you’ll gently uncover the hidden patterns that have been keeping you stuck. Together, we will soften the grip of self-sabotage and awaken the powerful, luminous woman within you. Here’s what you’ll experience:

Reframe Self-Doubt

Learn how to lovingly quiet the inner critic that says you’re not worthy. You’ll nurture a new, compassionate inner voice—one that speaks to you with encouragement, belief, and self-honoring.

Transform Limiting Beliefs

With tenderness and awareness, you’ll identify the old beliefs that have kept you small—and gently release them. You’ll learn how to open your mind and heart to new possibilities, creating space for genuine growth and empowerment.

Step Into Your Power

By the end of this journey, you’ll feel more connected to your true desires, ready to take aligned, courageous action. No more shrinking back—only expansion, self-trust, and the joy of living your truth.

This is your season of rising. It’s time to shed the old stories, embrace your inner strength, and honor the woman you are becoming.

Meet Alina

Alina isn’t just a guide—she’s a compassionate soul devoted to walking alongside you on your path to empowerment and joy. A certified Life Coach and NLP Master Practitioner, Alina blends her deep love for languages and cultures with a passion for helping women reconnect to their inner brilliance.

At 32, Alina shares a beautiful life with her husband, an Army Aviator, and their two sweet pups, making a home wherever love and adventure call them—currently in cozy Alabama. Her journey has taken her from teaching English, Russian, Italian, and German to living in Italy, where she earned her Master’s in Linguistics. Through it all, she discovered her true calling: guiding women to transform their mindset, trust themselves deeply, and create lives that feel rich with possibility.

You are so ready, beautiful soul. Let’s begin this journey together.
