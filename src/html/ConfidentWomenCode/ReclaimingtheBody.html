Lesson 3: Reclaiming the Body: Somatic Confidence & Breathwork

Confidence isn’t just something you think about—it’s something you feel in every fiber of your being.

Sure, you can say affirmations, shift your mindset, and reframe your thoughts all day long. But if your body doesn’t feel safe, grounded, and powerful beneath it all, your nervous system will quietly—sometimes loudly—undermine every good intention you have.

This week, we’re coming home to your body in a whole new way. Not as a thing to be critiqued or controlled, but as a sacred vessel holding your deepest wisdom, your sense of safety, and your birthright of sovereignty.

It’s time to stop trying to think your way into confidence. Instead, we’re going to slow down, breathe deeply, and embodyit feeling the strength that’s always been inside you, even when you couldn’t quite reach it before.

Your power isn’t out there somewhere waiting to be found. It’s alive, breathing, and pulsing through your body right now. This week, let’s awaken it softly, powerfully, and with intention—so you can stand tall in your truth and move through the world with fierce, grounded confidence.

Understand: How Confidence Lives in the Body

Confidence isn’t some distant prize you chase after it’s something you activate from within.

It’s not just a thought or an idea; it’s alive in your breath, in the way you hold yourself, in the tone and strength of your voice, and in the fullness of your presence. Yet, over time, life’s hard knocks trauma, shame, chronic stress can quietly disconnect us from this wellspring of power and intuition that lives in our bodies.

This week is about reconnecting with that truth. You’ll explore how your nervous system carries the story of your confidence how past experiences shape the way you hold yourself today, often without you even realizing it.

You’ll discover the deep link between emotional safety and what it feels like to fully express yourself in your body and in your life.

And here’s something so important to remember: when you notice yourself shrinking, holding tension, or collapsing inside that’s not who you are. Those are survival responses your body developed to keep you safe. Now, with compassion and awareness, you can begin to gently shift those patterns and create new, empowering habits of being.

Because here’s the truth: when you reclaim your body, you reclaim everything that makes you you your voice, your instincts, your pleasure, your fire.

This week, you’re invited to begin standing tall in your truth not just in your mind, but literally in your body and energetically in the world. It’s time to step into that embodied confidence that’s always been waiting for you.

Gentle Somatic Practice to Connect with Your Confidence

Take a moment now to pause and find a comfortable space whether sitting or standing.

1. Close your eyes and take a deep breath in through your nose, filling your belly fully.

2. Exhale slowly through your mouth, releasing any tension you notice in your body.

3. Bring your attention to your posture feel your feet rooted to the ground, your spine lengthening, your shoulders softening but open.

4. Place one hand over your heart and the other over your belly. Feel the rise and fall of your breath here the steady rhythm of your life and power.

5. Silently or aloud, say to yourself: “I am safe in my body. I am rooted, I am strong. My confidence lives within me and grows with every breath.”

6. IImagine a warm, glowing light spreading from your heart down through your body, awakening your strength, your fire, your voice.

Stay here for a few moments, simply being with that feeling of grounded confidence.

When you’re ready, open your eyes carrying that sense of power with you as you move through your day.

Embody: Practices for Presence & Personal Power

This isn’t about quick fixes or surface-level exercises. This is about stepping fully into your body as the sacred vessel of your power—cultivating confidence from the inside out, in a way that feels authentic, alive, and deeply nourishing.

Power Posture Work

Your body is a living, breathing signal to the world—and more importantly, to yourself. How you hold yourself shapes your biochemistry, colors your emotional landscape, and radiates your leadership energy. Here, we dive into cultivating a posture that speaks your truth: expansive heart, grounded hips, and a lifted crown. When you embody this stance, you’re not just “standing tall” you’re anchoring into the truth that you belong exactly where you are. No need to shrink, no need to apologize. Your presence is your power.

Somatic Release Techniques

Emotions, tension, and stress often get stuck in the body, quietly holding us back from our fullest expression. Through gentle shaking, primal vocal toning, and intuitive movement, you’ll learn how to shake off what no longer serves you. These aren’t random moves — they’re ancient, embodied practices that release trapped energy and invite you home to your aliveness. You’ll feel lighter, freer, and more connected to the vibrant life pulsing within you.

Feminine Embodiment Rituals

This is a sacred invitation to drop beneath your mind and into the luscious wisdom of your body. Here, you’ll awaken your sensuality, deepen your intuition, and cultivate a magnetic presence that flows effortlessly from within. Your body is not something to fix or control—it’s your most intimate ally, a living temple where your essence dances. Through ritual, movement, and mindful presence, you reclaim your feminine power in a way that feels both wild and tender, fierce and gentle.

When you embody these practices, you don’t just look confident—you are confidence. Your power isn’t something you have to chase; it’s always been inside you, waiting to be unlocked and honored. This is your invitation to step into that embodied power and live from a place of wholehearted presence.

Breathe: Breathwork to Regulate, Awaken & Energize

Your breath is one of the most profound tools you have—quiet, always with you, and deeply powerful. It’s your personal gateway to calming overwhelm, shifting your energy, and stepping fully into your power.

This week, I’m inviting you to come home to your breath in a whole new way. You’ll experience breathwork practices designed to meet you exactly where you are:

Calming Breathwork

When anxiety, overwhelm, or that frozen “stuck” feeling tries to take over, these gentle breath techniques will soothe your nervous system, helping you find ease and softness inside—even when the outside feels chaotic.

Activating Breathwork

Ready to ignite your clarity, focus, and that bold, embodied confidence? These energizing breath patterns will help you tap into your inner fire, fueling your courage to speak up, show up, and shine bright.

Centering Breath Rituals

Before stepping into moments that feel vulnerable—whether it’s a tough conversation, a big presentation, or simply being seen—you’ll have simple, powerful rituals to ground and root yourself into your power. These breaths help you stop outsourcing your power and anchor fully in the present moment, where your truest, most radiant self lives.

Breath is your anchor. When you change your breath, you change your state. You shift from reaction to presence. From overwhelm to clarity. From doubt to fierce confidence.

This week is your invitation to reclaim this sacred tool—to embody your power with every inhale and exhale, and to carry that centered strength wherever you go.

Simple Breath Practice: The 4-7-8 Reset

Find a comfortable seat, and when you’re ready, try this together:

  • Breathe in slowly through your nose for a count of 4. Feel your belly expand, filling with calm energy.
  • Hold your breath gently for a count of 7. Let this be a pause of grounded stillness—your body’s way of settling and centering.
  • Exhale fully through your mouth for a count of 8,releasing tension, doubt, and any tightness you’re holding. Imagine breathing out what no longer serves you.

Repeat this cycle 3-5 times, noticing how your body softens and your mind quiets. Feel yourself stepping into a calmer, clearer, more confident space.

Carry this breath with you throughout your day. Whenever you feel scattered, overwhelmed, or unsure, return to this breath. It’s your secret tool for reclaiming calm and power—always available, always within you.

You’ll start by gently moving your body, stretching into consciousness, and inviting life and energy into every muscle, every joint. This isn’t about forcing or pushing—it's about listening to your body and honoring what it needs to feel alive and supported.

Empowered Reminder

Listen closely: you don’t need to fake confidence—you already have it.

It’s been quietly stored inside your bones, in the rhythm of your breath, in the deep knowing of your belly. Waiting patiently. Watching lovingly. Ready for you to remember it.

This week, you’re done chasing power outside yourself. Instead, you’ll begin to feel it moving through you—rising up with every breath, every movement, every steady step you take.

Your body isn’t the problem. It’s your greatest ally. Your sacred path back to yourself.
