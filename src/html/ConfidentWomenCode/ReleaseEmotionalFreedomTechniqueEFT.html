Lesson 2: Release: Emotional Freedom Technique (EFT): Tapping Into Your Power to Heal

EFT, or tapping, is a gentle yet profoundly transformative tool that meets you right where you are—body, mind, and spirit. It’s not about pushing harder or “just thinking positive.” It’s about connecting deeply with your nervous system and the energy held within your body, and then lovingly releasing what no longer serves you.

When your Inner Critic kicks in, it often triggers that old, familiar fight-flight-freeze response. Your heart races, your thoughts spiral, and your body tenses—as if danger is lurking. EFT works by using simple, soothing taps on key acupressure points, paired with intentional words that acknowledge and honor your experience. This combo helps to literally calm your nervous system in real time.

This week, you’ll learn how to:

  • Tune into the emotional charge behind your Inner Critic’s voice whether it’s shame, fear, or frustration—and give it a safe place to be felt and seen. Because what’s acknowledged begins to lose its power.
  • Gently tap through those charged emotions and limiting storiesthat have been stored in your body and energy field, clearing stuck patterns and creating space for ease, confidence, and self-compassion.
  • Rewrite your relationship with anxiety, self-judgment, and perfectionism not by battling or ignoring them, but by transforming their energy so they no longer hold you hostage.

This isn’t just a quick fix. It’s a form of energetic repatterning rewiring your emotional and nervous system responses at a deep, cellular level. And the best part? It’s a skill you can carry with you for life, giving you immediate access to calm and clarity whenever your Inner Critic tries to steal your power.

You’re about to step into a new way of being—with more grace, presence, and authority over your internal world. EFT is one of the keys that will help you unlock that freedom.

EFT Tapping Script to Calm Your Inner Critic

Step 1: Setup Statement (Repeat 3 times while tapping the Karate Chop point — the side of your hand)

“Even though my Inner Critic is telling me I’m not enough, I deeply and completely love and accept myself.”

(Feel free to tweak this to fit what you’re feeling)

Step 2: Tap through the points below, saying the reminder phrase at each spot (Use 5-7 gentle taps on each point)

  • Eyebrow (EB):“This Inner Critic voice…”
  • Side of Eye (SE):“All these harsh judgments…”
  • Under Eye (UE):“The fear of not being enough…”
  • Under Nose (UN):“The pressure to be perfect…”
  • Chin (CH):“The voice that tries to hold me back…”
  • Collarbone (CB):“I’m ready to release this critical energy…”
  • Under Arm (UA):“I choose to calm this voice with love and kindness…”
  • Top of Head (TH):“I am safe, I am enough, just as I am.”

Step 3: Take a deep breath, and check in with your Inner Critic’s intensity (on a scale from 0 to 10). If it’s still high, repeat the tapping sequence with slight variations in your reminder phrases like:

  • “Even though part of me still doubts, I’m open to feeling calm.”
  • “I’m learning to meet this voice with compassion.”
  • “I am choosing my peace over this criticism.”

Step 4: Close by affirming your power and self-love Tap the Karate Chop point again and say: “I am worthy. I am powerful. I am more than my Inner Critic.”

Tips for your practice:

  • You can do this tapping anywhere before a meeting, when anxiety rises, or just as a daily ritual.
  • Speak your own truth. Customize the phrases to what feels real and authentic to you.
  • Remember: The goal is not to silence the critic but to transform your relationship with it.

Real-Time Tapping for Anxiety + Comparison

Because the critic often shows up when you’re about to expand speak up, be seen, set boundaries, raise your standards you’ll learn to use tapping in real time to:

  • Move through spirals of overthinking or comparison
  • Ground yourself before visibility moments (public speaking, social media, boundary setting, etc.)
  • Shift out of “I’m not ready” into “I’m grounded, worthy, and enough right now.”

EFT will become your on-the-spot tool for reclaiming your power and peace.

Guided EFT Tapping Session to Calm Your Inner Critic

Step 1: Setup Statement

Tap gently on the side of your hand (the Karate Chop point) with your fingertips, and repeat after me or say your own version that feels true: “Even though my Inner Critic is loud right now, I deeply and completely love and accept myself.” Repeat this 2 more times with calm, steady tapping.

Step 2: Tap through the points with me — 5-7 gentle taps per spot.

Eyebrow:

“This voice inside that judges me…”

Side of Eye:

“All the harsh things it says…”

Under Eye:

“The fear it stirs up in me…”

Under Nose:

“The pressure to be perfect…”

Chin:

“The stories it tells to keep me small…”

Collarbone:

“I’m starting to see this voice with kindness…”

Under Arm:

“I choose to soften its hold on me…”

Top of Head:

“I am safe, I am enough, I am whole.”

Step 3: Take a deep breath in... and out...

Pause for a moment and notice how you feel. If the intensity of your Inner Critic is still present, that’s okay. We’ll gently repeat with some different words.

Step 4: Tap again, same points.

Eyebrow:

“It’s okay to feel this way…”

Side of Eye:

“I’m learning to hold this voice with compassion…”

Under Eye:

“I’m choosing peace over fear…”

Under Nose:

“I’m letting go of what no longer serves me…”

Chin:

“I deserve love and kindness…”

Collarbone:

“I am stepping into my power…”

Under Arm:

“My voice is valid and worthy…”

Top of Head:

“I am calm, I am strong, I am free.”

Step 5: Close with the Karate Chop point

Tap gently and say:

“I am enough, exactly as I am. I am reclaiming my power with love and grace.”

When you’re ready, take a few slow, grounding breaths. Notice how your body feels, how your mind feels. Remember, every time you tap, you’re rewiring your nervous system and inviting a new story to unfold.

Practice: Confidence, Criticism & Visibility Tapping

This week, I’m inviting you to a sacred daily ritual designed to gently clear the three biggest blocks that keep you from stepping fully into your power and expressing your true self:

ConfidenceTogether, we’ll tap away the fear, the second-guessing, and those nagging doubts that dim your light. You’ll learn to anchor deep within your soul the truth of your innate worthiness because you are enough, just as you are.

CriticismWe’ll work through the heavy layers of internalized shame, harsh self judgment, and the echo of others’ voices that have tried to tell you who you should be. This is your chance to release those weights, and instead, invite in compassion, kindness, and radical self-acceptance.

Empowered Reminder

Sweetheart, remember this: You are not the voice that doubts.

That critical voice? The one that whispers “not enough,” “what if you fail,” or “who do you think you are?” it’s just a part of your experience, not the whole story. And you, beautiful soul, are so much more than that noise.

You are the woman strong enough to hear that voice and, with gentle strength, lovingly invite it to speak and then set it free.

Your Inner Critic doesn’t get to define your worth, your dreams, or your power. What truly defines you is your willingness to show up fully present, with open arms, and a heart wide enough to hold all of it: the fear, the shame, the doubts... and the fierce courage that rises in response.

This week, you’re stepping into emotional freedom. You’re learning that rising isn’t about shutting down or silencing that inner voice. No, rising happens when you lead her with kindness, with compassion, and with the embodied courage of a woman who knows her value and refuses to shrink.

You are unstoppable because you meet yourself with love always. And that love is your power.
