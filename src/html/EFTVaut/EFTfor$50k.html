Lesson 5: EFT for $50k

I want to begin by honoring the part of you that showed up for this practice — the part of you that dares to believe in more. More ease. More space. More freedom. More abundance.

Whether you’re here out of longing, necessity, hope, or curiosity — it all matters. You matter. And today, we’re not just calling in a number. We’re calling in a frequency — one of deserving, receptivity, safety, and embodied worth.

This is a sacred recalibration. One that gently dissolves the internal blocks, the inherited stories, and the ancestral fear around money… so your nervous system can actually feel safe receiving $50,000 and beyond.

Anchoring the Body: Breath & Presence

We begin with breath — the bridge between your body and your spirit.

Place one hand over your heart, and the other over your solar plexus — your center of personal power. Soften your shoulders. Unclench your jaw. Invite in stillness.

Let’s take three slow, nurturing breaths:

  • Inhale through the nose... Feel the breath expand your chest, your belly, your back. Hold for 3… 2… 1… Exhale softly through the mouth.
  • Inhale abundance, possibility, openness. Hold for 3… 2… 1… Exhale doubt, constriction, shame.
  • One more deep breath in… Imagine it carrying light into every cell of your body. Hold… and gently exhale.

You are here. You are safe. You are worthy.

Tapping Practice: Beginning with the Karate Chop Point

Tap gently on the side of your hand (karate chop point) while repeating after me — or simply absorb the energy of the words:

Even though I don’t see $50,000 in my bank account right now…
And part of me feels unworthy or unsure if I ever will…
I love and accept myself exactly as I am.

Even though I desire this money deeply,
a part of me is afraid of what it would mean to actually have it.
I honor that fear with compassion.
And I am open to healing it — gently, lovingly, at my own pace.

Even though I’ve carried limiting beliefs about money for years…
I choose to believe that another way is possible.
I’m ready to feel safe with abundance.
I’m ready to receive.

Tapping Through the Points: Honoring the Resistance

  • Eyebrow Point: There’s a part of me that doubts this is possible.
  • Side of the Eye: A part of me says, “It’s too much… who am I to ask for that?”
  • Under the Eye: Maybe I learned that money is hard to earn… or dangerous to want.
  • Under the Nose: I carry old stories — family beliefs, societal rules, ancestral wounds.
  • Chin Point: I’ve been taught to shrink. To settle. To struggle.
  • Collarbone Point: I feel the tension in my body when I think about having that much money.
  • Under the Arm: I’m afraid I’ll lose it. Or that it will change me.
  • Top of the Head: What if it’s safe to want this? What if it’s sacred to allow this?

Tapping Into Worthiness & Receiving

  • Eyebrow Point: I am ready to release the fear that says I’m not enough.
  • Side of the Eye: I am not here to repeat old scarcity cycles.
  • Under the Eye: I am ready to feel safe in my body while receiving more.
  • Under the Nose: This money is a resource — not a threat.
  • Chin Point: It will support my healing, my vision, my joy.
  • Collarbone Point: I don’t have to know how. I just have to say yes.
  • Under the Arm: Yes to allowing. Yes to trusting. Yes to receiving with grace.
  • Top of the Head: I am becoming the version of me who feels free around money.

Tapping into Abundance Consciousness

$50,000 is not just a number. It’s a doorway. Into expansion. Into deeper service. Into resourced rest. Into pleasure without guilt.

I am open to receiving this amount — and more — in ways that feel ethical, aligned, and joyful.

I let go of the pressure to earn my worth. I let go of the shame I’ve carried. I let go of the fear of being seen with wealth.

I choose wealth with integrity. I choose wealth as a healer. I choose wealth as a woman reclaiming her power.

Sacred Affirmations of Alignment

  • I am safe to receive.
  • I am a channel for divine abundance.
  • I allow money to flow to me, through me, and around me with love.
  • I am grounded in my values and supported by the universe.
  • I am no longer available for struggle as my baseline.
  • I say yes to ease. Yes to expansion. Yes to $50,000 and beyond.

Closing the Session

Place your hands back over your heart and belly. Let yourself pause. Feel. Integrate.

Take a final slow breath in… Hold… Exhale softly, fully, completely.

Check in with yourself now. How does your body feel? What sensations are present? If resistance was a 10 before, where is it now?

There’s no rush to “fix” anything — your healing unfolds in perfect timing.

Come back to this practice anytime your fear of lack resurfaces. Let this be your ritual of remembrance: that you were born worthy of beauty, softness, and overflowing support.

Final Blessing

Beloved,
You are not asking for too much.
You are remembering that you are already enough.

Let this be the season where you stop trying to earn your worth — and start allowing it to support you.

You are resourced.
You are radiant.
You are ready.

And $50,000 is just the beginning.

Nice work! This Lesson is complete.
