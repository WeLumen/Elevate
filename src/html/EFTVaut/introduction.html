Introduction

Welcome to your course EFT Vaut. This course is divided into 4 modules.

Module 1: Emotional Freedom Technique

In this foundational module, you’ll be introduced to the core principles of EFT and how it can support emotional liberation and nervous system regulation. You’ll begin by exploring what EFT truly is and how it works, then dive into targeted tapping sessions that address common emotional and energetic blocks. Together, we’ll gently clear money blocks, release feelings of powerlessness, transform people-pleasing patterns, and align with expansive financial goals. Each lesson offers an invitation to step into your personal power with greater ease and self-trust.

Module 2: EFT

This module focuses on restoring your sense of self-worth and releasing the deep-rooted belief that you’re not enough. Through guided tapping, you’ll begin to shift inner narratives, reconnect with your inherent value, and cultivate a more loving relationship with yourself. These practices will help you feel more grounded, deserving, and empowered in your everyday life.

Module 3: Tapping to Feel Calm

In this short yet soothing module, you’ll learn how to use EFT to find calm in moments of stress, overwhelm, or anxiety. With compassion and care, you’ll reconnect to your breath, your body, and the present moment, inviting peace back into your nervous system.

Module 4: Releasing Fear with EFT

The final module gently supports you in acknowledging and releasing fear — whether it's fear of failure, change, or simply the unknown. You’ll be guided to meet fear with presence and compassion, using EFT to soften its grip and open your heart to safety, trust, and courage.

Each module is a step toward emotional freedom, a deeper sense of worth, and a more empowered connection to yourself. May this course be a safe and sacred space for healing, growth, and self-liberation.
