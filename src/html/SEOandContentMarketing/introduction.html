Introduction

Welcome to your course SEO and Content Marketing. This course is divided into 4 modules.

Module 1

Dive into the fundamentals of search engine marketing. Understand how SEM works, explore different ad platforms, and learn to align your marketing objectives with search strategies.

Module 2

Deep dive into the essential components of SEO, from website structure and crawlability to content relevance and authority. You’ll build the foundation for long-term, sustainable search visibility.

Module 3

Learn how to track performance, identify opportunities, and make data-driven decisions. Explore key metrics, reporting tools, and optimization techniques that help you maximize impact.

Module 4

Wrap up your journey with a comprehensive review and strategic guidance on how to apply what you've learned. Discover practical next steps to implement your SEO and content marketing strategies in real-world settings.
