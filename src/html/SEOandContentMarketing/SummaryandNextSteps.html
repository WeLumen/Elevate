Lesson 1: Summary and Next Steps

Present and Future of SEO

SEO is a constantly evolving field due to frequent updates to search algorithms and changes in user behaviour. Today, factors such as user experience, content quality, keyword relevance, and page load speed play a fundamental role in a website’s ranking.

In the future (and already in the present), artificial intelligence and machine learning are expected to have a greater impact on SEO. Google already uses these technologies to improve the accuracy of its search results, and their importance is only set to grow in the coming years.

Now, Google can provide quick answers to a large number of queries without the need to visit any web page (e.g., "Taylor Swift's age"). However, the challenge lies in being able to generate fast answers through artificial intelligence.

Moreover, search from other platforms like Instagram or TikTok is becoming more popular, which means SEO professionals will need to adapt to these new ways of searching.

A focus on mobile devices will also remain crucial, as over 60 % of searches are made from mobile phones (in your own project, this figure might even exceed 80 %). Therefore, SEO strategies must prioritise mobile optimisation (Mobile-First) and improve the user experience on these devices.

    • Conclusions

    SEO continues to be one of the most effective digital marketing strategies for attracting qualified traffic to a website. Search engine optimisation requires a continuous and adaptable approach, taking into account frequent search algorithm updates and changes in user behaviour.

    Additionally, in case you didn't know, 15% of the searches conducted on Google each day are new.

    SEO strategy is sometimes viewed as a technical discipline, with tactics that are closer to "tricking" or "hacking" an algorithm than generating business for the brand itself. However, nothing could be further from the truth—an effective SEO strategy helps to enhance other areas of the business, such as design, content, or technology, and thus has a greater impact on the bottom line.

    It is important to stay updated on SEO trends and best practices, as well as to use analysis and monitoring tools to constantly evaluate and adjust SEO strategies. Artificial Intelligence is changing how SEO is done. There is still much to discover, so don't miss out on its potential uses and applications.
