Introduction

Welcome to your course HealHer. This course is divided into 14 modules.

Module 1: Inner Child Healing

This journey is a beautiful and transformative exploration of self-discovery and emotional healing. We begin by reconnecting with our inner child, using practices like meditation and compassionate listening to heal past wounds. We then gently confront past traumas, taking it one step at a time and recognizing when professional support is needed. Reparenting ourselves through self-care and nurturing creativity becomes an essential tool in honoring our inner child. To maintain emotional health, we learn to set healthy boundaries and engage in ongoing self-reflection. When challenges arise, we embrace our emotions with kindness and practice self-compassion while building resilience. Each lesson is an invitation to transform, empowering us to deepen our personal growth and emotional healing. This is a journey of patience, self-love, and the beautiful process of embracing who we truly are.

Module 2: Shadow Work

Shadow Work is a journey of self-exploration that helps you embrace and integrate the hidden aspects of yourself with love and compassion. For the healed woman, it is a powerful tool for self-awareness, transformation, and emotional freedom. Through journaling, guided reflections, and rituals, you will uncover and heal repressed emotions, release limiting beliefs, and cultivate a deeper connection with your authentic self.

Module 3: Flower Healing

Flower Healing harnesses the natural energies of flowers to restore emotional balance and promote well-being. This module explores how their vibrational properties can help release blockages, enhance self-awareness, and cultivate inner harmony. You will also dive into Bach Flower Therapy, a holistic system that uses flower essences to address emotional imbalances and support personal growth.
