Introduction

Welcome to your course E-Commerce with Commercial Management. This course is divided into 3 modules.

Module 1

Sales Management in the New Normal introduces the strategic foundations of sales leadership. Participants will explore the evolving role of sales management, evaluate the traits of effective salespeople, tackle real-world business management challenges, and learn how to set meaningful objectives for sales visits.

Module 2

The Personal Selling Process provides a step-by-step framework for building strong client relationships and achieving sales success. From the first impression to identifying client needs, presenting tailored solutions, and confidently closing deals, this module delivers practical techniques to enhance every stage of the personal selling journey.

Module 3

Integrating Digital Retailing into the New Normal focuses on the urgent need for digital transformation in sales. Participants will learn how to establish a strong online presence, manage business growth in digital environments, choose between selling on proprietary platforms or marketplaces, and implement effective change management strategies to support digital sales efforts.
