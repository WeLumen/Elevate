Module 7: Reframing Your Belief System

← Back to module overview

Lesson 5: Module 4 Workbook & Journal

Let’s gently travel to a future version of you... the one who lovingly released old patterns, embraced her worth, and is now living in deep alignment with her soul’s truth.

Step 1: Visualize Your Future Self

Close your eyes, slow your breath, and imagine yourself in a future moment where you’ve already overcome the habits that once held you back. Feel it. Sense it. Allow your whole being to experience the lightness, the pride, the joy of who you’ve become.

Step 2: Embody That Vision

  • Notice... what helped you get there?
  • What new beliefs did you embrace?
  • What actions nourished your growth?

Let this future version of you whisper her wisdom back to you. She knows the way.

Step 3: Bring the Wisdom Back

Now gently return to the present moment, carrying with you the resources, insights, and energy of your empowered future self.

Step 4: Anchor the Energy

Choose a simple gesture (like placing your hand on your heart) or a loving affirmation (like “I am becoming her”) to anchor this new version of you. Let this become your sacred cue, your reminder of what’s already unfolding within.

Step 5: Practice Consistency

Return to this practice often. Let it become a ritual of remembrance. Each time you anchor in your future self, you realign with your inner truth and deepest potential.

Step 6: Root into Belief

Know in your heart:

  • Your healing is happening.
  • Your growth is unfolding.
  • Your future self already exists — she’s simply waiting for you to step into her.

Step 7: Emotional Awareness Check-In

Pause and gently feel into your emotional landscape. Where are you right now, dear one?

Zone Emotional Vibes
Green Calm, grounded, open, in flow
Yellow Anxious, excited, scattered, unsure
Blue Sad, tired, low energy, vulnerable
Red Angry, panicked, out of control, overwhelmed

Whatever you notice, honor it. It’s okay to feel. Awareness is your first step to empowerment.

Stuck to Empowered Shift

Let’s lovingly unravel any limiting thoughts...

  1. Set a timer for 3 minutes. Gently write down every thought that comes up. Let it flow, without judgment.
  2. Now softly read them. Which ones feel heavy or painful?
  3. Let’s question them — not with criticism, but with curiosity:
  • What story is this thought telling me?
  • Is it absolutely true?
  • Is there another way to see this?
  • What’s the cost of holding onto it?
  • What could open up if I gently let it go?
  • Is this thought helping me live in alignment with my highest self?
  • What choice can I make today that brings me closer to peace?

Time Line Therapy®: Releasing the Past

Let’s create sacred space for release...

Step 1: Identify the Limitation

Write down one limiting belief or emotion you’re ready to release. Let it be clear. Let it be honest.

Step 2: Go Back Gently

With eyes closed and heart open, allow yourself to drift back to the first memory connected to this belief. There’s no need to force it. Trust what surfaces. Just witness.

Step 3: Observe with Compassion

Step outside of the memory. See it from a safe distance, as a kind and gentle observer. You’re not there anymore. You’re safe now.

Step 4: Gather the Lessons

Ask yourself: What did this experience teach me? What wisdom can I carry forward, now that I’m ready to let the pain go?

Step 5: Release with Intention

Imagine placing that limiting belief in a container — a box, a bubble, a leaf floating downstream. Watch it go, knowing it no longer belongs to you.

Step 6: Rewrite the Past with a New Future

Now float above your timeline. See your past, your present, and your radiant future. At the moment the old belief was born, gently place your new empowering belief there. Let it ripple forward in time.

Step 7: Step Into Your Future

See your future self — vibrant, free, and empowered. How does she move? How does she speak? Who is she becoming? You are already becoming her.

Step 8: Integrate the Shift

Float back into the present moment, bringing with you the light and clarity of your future self. Let it settle gently into your body.

Step 9: Ground & Reflect

Open your eyes. Take a breath. Notice: What shifted? What do you feel now?

Step 10: Celebrate Your Inner Work

This journey is powerful. It may take time and repetition. But each time you return, you’re choosing yourself. You’re reclaiming your power.

Small Wins Celebration

  • What felt good?
  • What felt aligned?
  • What small victories are worth honoring?
Win Impact on Me Feeling Next Step
Morning walk in nature More clarity and calm Connected Schedule more walks this week

Every small step is a sign of your commitment to healing. Let yourself feel proud.

Consistency Calendar: Honoring Your Commitments

Let’s gently track the habits that nourish your self-trust.

  • What supportive habits would you like to deepen this month?
  • Which ones help you feel grounded, empowered, connected?
  • Mark your calendar lovingly each time you follow through.
  • Reflect each week with tenderness, not pressure.

This is not about perfection. This is about presence.

Affirmations for Rewriting Limiting Beliefs

Repeat them aloud or in silence. Feel them in your body. Breathe them into your cells.

  1. I am at peace with who I am.
  2. Life supports me in every possible way.
  3. I am deserving of my dreams.
  4. I honor my own pace and timing.
  5. My intuition leads me to the most fruitful opportunities.
  6. I can handle anything that comes my way.
  7. I respect my own boundaries.
  8. I am brave enough to listen to my heart.
  9. My voice matters and I speak with love.
  10. I am healing, growing, and becoming.
  11. I let go of what no longer serves me.
  12. I am enough — always have been, always will be.
  13. I trust my path, even when I don’t see the full picture.
  14. I welcome abundance, ease, and joy.
  15. I choose to believe in myself, again and again.
