Lesson 1: Self-Awareness

Beloved, have you ever whispered to yourself, “Who am I… really?”

Do you feel like the radiant, grounded woman you know you are deep inside has been tucked away, waiting for a moment of remembrance?

Perhaps your days feel like a blur—filled with choices and routines that don't quite feel like you. You sense there's more to your life, more to your story… but the clarity feels just out of reach.

Maybe you've set intentions with the best of hopes, only to feel untethered, unsure of what truly lights you up from within.

Or maybe you're simply moving through the motions, longing to feel more alive, more connected, more you—ready to break free from autopilot and return home to your own sacred rhythm.

If your heart whispered yes to any of this, you’re in the right place.

This gentle, empowering self-awareness mini-course was lovingly created for The Reclaimed Woman—for the woman ready to return to herself with grace, courage, and compassion. Inside this sacred container, you will be guided to:

  • Quiet the Noise - Learn to soften the external voices and reconnect with the quiet, wise whisper of your soul. Begin to hear, clearly and lovingly, what you truly want and need.
  • Deepen Self-Awareness - Explore simple yet powerful tools to gently observe your thoughts, feelings, and patterns—without judgment. From this space of loving awareness, you’ll begin to make choices that feel aligned and empowering.
  • Rewrite Your Story - Meet the old narratives that have kept you small with kindness—and begin to weave a new story. One that honors the woman you are becoming: present, powerful, and deeply attuned to her inner truth.

By the end of our time together, you’ll feel more anchored in your truth and more confident in choosing a life that feels vibrant, nourishing, and beautifully you.

This is your invitation, love. To remember who you are. To reclaim your light. To walk forward with clarity, purpose, and deep self-trust.

You’re not lost—you’re coming home.
