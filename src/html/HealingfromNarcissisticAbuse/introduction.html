Introduction

Welcome to your course Healing from Narcissistic Abuse. This course is divided into 6 modules.

Module 1: The Path to Healing: A Journey of Self-Rediscovery

This foundational module guides you through the essential steps of healing from narcissistic abuse. You will explore what narcissistic abuse truly entails and understand the science behind trauma recovery. Learn how to heal your nervous system, reconnect with your inner child, and transform emotional pain into personal power. This module also focuses on setting healthy boundaries, rebuilding your self-worth, and restoring emotional and spiritual balance. By the end, you'll be equipped with practical tools to move forward confidently on your healing journey.

Module 2: You Are Not Alone And You Are Stronger Than You Know

In this empowering module, you’ll be reminded that your experiences are valid and that you are never alone in your healing process. This lesson focuses on building your inner strength and resilience, providing encouragement and support to help you recognize your inherent power to overcome challenges.

Module 3: Narcissistic Personality Disorder: A Trauma-Informed Exploration

Gain a deeper understanding of narcissistic personality disorder through a trauma-informed lens. This module explores the psychological mechanisms behind narcissism, differentiating between overt and covert narcissistic behaviors, and helps you see how these patterns emerge from underlying wounds. This knowledge will give you clarity and insight into the dynamics that affect your healing.

Module 4: Why Narcissists Hurt Others: A Holistic View

Explore the root causes of harmful narcissistic behaviors in this holistic module. Understand the hidden pain, trauma, and emotional deficits that fuel destructive patterns. This perspective helps you separate the abuser’s actions from your own worth and lays the groundwork for compassion without enabling.

Module 5: How Understanding Narcissistic Personality Disorder Supports

This module highlights the importance of education and awareness in your healing journey. By understanding narcissistic personality disorder, you gain tools to protect yourself, set boundaries, and foster recovery. Knowledge becomes a key ally in reclaiming your autonomy and breaking free from harmful cycles.

Module 6: The Many Faces of Narcissistic Abuse

Dive deep into the diverse tactics and behaviors used in narcissistic abuse. This comprehensive module covers:

  • Emotional and psychological abuse
  • Gaslighting and reality manipulation
  • Love-bombing and idealization
  • Devaluation and constant criticism
  • Silent treatment and emotional withholding
  • Triangulation and creating jealousy
  • Projection and blame-shifting
  • Financial abuse and control
  • Smear campaigns and character assassination
  • Hoovering and pulling you back in

Recognizing these patterns empowers you to identify abuse, validate your experience, and develop strategies for safety and healing.
