Lesson 1: How Understanding Narcissistic Personality Disorder Supports Your Healing

A trauma-informed guide to reclaiming your clarity, self-trust, and power

1. Grandiosity & Superiority Complex

What it looks like:

  • Overt narcissists flaunt achievements and dominate conversations.
  • Covert narcissists show superiority through passive disdain or martyrdom.
  • May play the victim or act like misunderstood geniuses to create guilt or obligation.

Holistic Awareness: Their superiority is a shield for shame. You were never meant to compete—you were meant to stand in your own light.

2. Lack of Empathy

What it looks like:

  • Overt: Dismisses emotions—“Get over it.”
  • Covert: Uses false compassion or shifts focus back to themselves.
  • Leaves you feeling alone and invalidated.

Holistic Awareness: Their disconnection isn’t your fault. Offer the compassion you needed—to yourself.

3. Gaslighting & Reality Manipulation

What it looks like:

  • Overt: Aggressively denies reality—“You’re insane.”
  • Covert: Subtly undermines—“You’re too emotional.”
  • Causes confusion and self-doubt over time.

Holistic Awareness: Keep records, trust your body, and talk to safe people. You’re not crazy—you’re waking up.

4. Exploitation & Using Others for Gain

What it looks like:

  • Overt: Openly transactional—uses people for gain.
  • Covert: Wraps exploitation in guilt or flattery.
  • Promises recognition but offers none.

Holistic Awareness: Reciprocity is the mark of healthy connection. Ask: “Would this relationship exist if I stopped giving?”

5. Extreme Need for Control

What it looks like:

  • Overt: Makes blunt demands—control over clothing, passwords, etc.
  • Covert: “I’m just protecting you,” while isolating you.
  • Doubts your choices under the guise of concern.

Holistic Awareness: Boundaries are freedom. Redefine love as safe—not suffocating.

6. Victim Mindset & Blame-Shifting

What it looks like:

  • Overt: Loudly blames others—“Everyone is out to get me!”
  • Covert: Subtle guilt—“I guess I’m always the bad guy.”
  • Manipulates emotions to avoid accountability.

Holistic Awareness: You are not cruel for wanting clarity. Let go of the fixer role—you deserve honest relationships.

7. Hot & Cold Behavior (Idealization & Devaluation)

What it looks like:

  • Overt: Love bombs, then withdraws or criticizes.
  • Covert: Shows deep emotion, then disappears emotionally or gives backhanded compliments.
  • Creates confusion and craving for the “early” version of them.

Holistic Awareness: Real love is steady. Choose yourself instead of chasing cycles meant to confuse.

8. Intense Jealousy & Need for Constant Admiration

What it looks like:

  • Overt: Accuses you of cheating, seeks constant validation.
  • Covert: Minimizes your wins or withdraws when you shine.
  • Uses subtle tactics to keep admiration flowing their way.

Holistic Awareness: You don’t need to shrink to be loved. Shine anyway—the right people will celebrate you.

9. Discarding & Sudden Withdrawal

What it looks like:

  • Overt: Abruptly ends relationships, flaunts replacements.
  • Covert: Quietly fades out with vague excuses, but quickly replaces you.
  • May return later as if nothing happened.

Holistic Awareness: Their absence makes room for your presence to rise. You don’t need their closure—your healing is enough.
