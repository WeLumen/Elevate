Lesson 4: Somatic Healing Meditaiton

A Guided Practice for Embodied Awareness & Inner Connection

This is a gentle somatic meditation. Begin by settling into a position that feels comfortable and supported. Allow your body the space to shift, fidget, or adjust. Trust your body to guide you to a place of ease. Comfort is foundational to this work.

Breath Awareness & Grounding

Start by bringing attention to your breath.

  • Inhale slowly through your nose… notice the air as it enters.
  • Pause briefly at the top of the breath.
  • Exhale gently through your mouth… and soften.

With each breath, feel yourself becoming more grounded—more connected to the Earth. Allow yourself to be held by the support beneath you. Feel the weight of your body sinking gently, rooted in the present moment.

Body Scan with Awareness

Begin to bring gentle awareness to the crown of your head. This is the starting point of your journey through the body—a path of presence, curiosity, and compassion. As your awareness moves, simply notice what arises. There is no right or wrong. Allow sensation, emotion, or stillness to be welcomed without judgment.

  • Hair & Scalp: Bring attention here. Notice the texture, the sensation. Rest your hands lightly if it feels supportive. Offer this part of the body your awareness.
  • Face: Gently tune in to your forehead, temples, cheeks, jaw, and lips. Use soft touch if you’d like. Notice if there’s tension to be released or tenderness that wants to be acknowledged. Invite relaxation. Allow the muscles to soften.
  • Throat: Direct awareness to your throat—the center of voice and expression. Observe if there’s any sensation here. Breathe into this space. On the exhale, you may release with a sigh, hum, or gentle sound to support release.
  • Heart Space: Place your hands over your chest. This area holds emotion, memory, and presence. Feel your heartbeat. Breathe into this space and allow anything present to be acknowledged—without the need to fix or change it.
  • Belly / Core: Rest your hands on your belly. Connect with your center—your space of intuition, creation, and emotional storage. Breathe deeply. Notice what arises. Invite this area to soften. Offer kindness to what is held here.
  • Legs & Feet: Travel down to your legs, knees, ankles, and feet. These are your roots. Bring gratitude to these parts of the body that carry you forward. Notice any tension or sensation. Gently acknowledge the strength and history stored here.

Energetic Releasing & Self-Touch

At any point during this journey, place your hands on any area of your body that calls for attention. This might be a space that feels heavy, tense, numb, or simply needs presence.

Silently or aloud, offer words of care:

  • “I see you.”
  • “You are safe with me.”
  • “I honor all that you’ve carried.”

Let your hands offer comfort and connection. This is the practice of reestablishing a trusting relationship with your body.

Integration: Intentional Energy Calling

Now, tune into what you are calling in—an energy, a word, or a feeling. It may be:

  • Peace
  • Wholeness
  • Clarity
  • Love
  • Belonging

See this energy entering your space—from above, from below, from within—filling every cell of your being. Let it flow freely through your body. Let it settle. Let it glow within you.

Closing Practice

To complete, make an inner promise—a commitment to yourself. Perhaps to listen more deeply. To rest more often. To meet your body with kindness. To allow healing to unfold at its own pace.

You may wish to place your hands over your heart… or gently wrap your arms around yourself.

Feel your breath. Feel your body. Feel your presence.

Know this: You are held. You are healing. You are whole.

Remain here for as long as you wish. This is your sacred space.

Nice work! This Lesson is complete.
