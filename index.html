DevelopHer

E-learning Courses

DevelopHer offers self-paced e-learning courses designed to help you start a small business or launch your career. Gain the skills, confidence, and strategies needed to turn your ambitions into success—on your own schedule.


This course is for you if you're ready to step into your power, build a thriving career or business, and create a life of confidence and purpose. Throughout this journey, you’ll gain practical tools, expert insights, and empowering strategies to help you grow, succeed, and lead with authenticity.

Course overview:

  • Academic
  • 12 Modules
  • Study Group Access
  • Earn reward points

What Will You Learn?

In this transformative journey, you will heal emotional wounds and reconnect with your authentic self through Inner Child Healing and Shadow Work, releasing limiting beliefs and patterns. You will develop deeper, more meaningful relationships by mastering conscious communication, fostering healthier connections. You will approach motherhood with intention and awareness, breaking generational cycles and creating a nurturing environment for both you and your children. By practicing Somatic techniques and Breathwork, you will learn to release stored emotions and restore physical and emotional balance.

You will gain insight into the natural hormonal changes in your body and learn how to navigate transitions like menopause with wisdom and self-care. Discover how holistic nutrition supports your well-being, while daily meditation and affirmations will help you cultivate inner peace, self-love, and resilience.

Lastly, you will shift limiting beliefs, build confidence, and develop a mindset that aligns with your highest potential.

Who Would Benefit from This Course?

This course is for women ready to heal emotional wounds, release limiting patterns, and build conscious, healthier relationships. It’s ideal for those wanting to embrace motherhood with awareness, manage stress and physical tension, understand hormonal changes, and nourish their bodies holistically. If you’re seeking to cultivate inner peace, boost self-love, and transform your mindset to live with purpose, this course is for you.
